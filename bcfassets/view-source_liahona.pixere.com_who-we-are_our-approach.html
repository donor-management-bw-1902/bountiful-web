Our Approach

We are a Global Nutrition Organization

We provide nutritional supplements to children in 18 countries around the
world using local leaders and local supplements, focusing on the first 1,000 days of life.

Once a child has been screened and identified as undernourished according
to World Health Organization growth charts, they are enrolled in our program.


This includes:


  • Micronutrient and calorie
    dense supplements

  • Growth monitoring
    every 6 months

  • Educating families on hygienic and healthy food preparation and meal planning.

Our health lessons promote exclusive breastfeeding until 6 months, and all pregnant and lactating mothers are enrolled in the program and given nutritional supplements. In areas where needed, we also provide semi-annual de-worming of children and elementary school scholarships.
